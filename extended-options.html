Extended Leave of Absences

Negotiated Agreement Disclosure

HCPSS employees should consult their negotiated agreement as it pertains to eligibility, duration and requirements for extended leaves of absence.

  • Howard County Education Association (HCEA) – Article 17
  • Education Support Professionals (ESP) – Article 7
  • American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees (AFSCME) – Articles 8 and 9
  • Howard County Administrators Association (HCAA) – Article 8
  • Food Service Association (FSA) - Article 6

View additional information on negotiated agreements →

Overview of Each Condition

Study/Professional

Required Documentation

  • Request Form
  • Benefits Billing Form
  • Verification: Documentation defining academic program and verifying acceptance into program
  • Maryland State Retirement Form - optional

Childrearing

Required Documentation

  • Request Form
  • Benefits Billing Form
  • Maryland State Retirement Form - optional and only if childrearing leave immediately follows an FMLA leave of absence.
  • Verification: Documentation verifying birth of child
