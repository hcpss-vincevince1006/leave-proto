Applying for a Leave of Absence

Qualifications

A request for leave of absence should be submitted to the Office of Human Resources when an HCPSS employee meets the following qualifications:

  • The employee has been absent more than 5 consecutive days of work
  • The employee is aware of an upcoming qualifying condition* that will require them to be absent for more than 5 consecutive days of work (temporary leave of absence)
  • The employee wishes to apply for an extended leave of absence

Leave of Absence Options

Temporary Leaves of Absence

Qualifying Conditions:

  • Maternity or Paternity
  • Personal Illness
  • Family Member Illness
  • Military
  • Adoption

Extended Leaves of Absence Options:

Qualifying Conditions:

  • Study/Professional

How do I apply for a leave of absence?

Note: Contacting your principal or supervisor (manager) does not qualify as a request or approval for a leave of absence. The Office of Human Resources must receive, review and approve all requests for leaves of absence.

Temporary Leaves of Absence:

Each qualifying condition requires a leave of absence request form, Healthcare provider certification form and a benefits billing form. Employees may elect to complete a Maryland State Retirement form.

Extended Leaves of Absence:

Each option for an extended leave of absence require an employee to complete a request for leave of absence including request form, benefits billing form and documentation verifying eligibility for request. Employees may elect to complete a Maryland State Retirement form.

Application Requirements

All forms must be completed and received by Human Resources before your leave will be approved.

Request for Leave of Absence form

Complete all personal information and select the type of leave you wish to use.

Healthcare Provider Form

Submit form to your healthcare provider. The form must have a beginning and end date for the duration of incapactiy.

Maryland State Retirement Form

Family member illness is not applicable. Only complete this form if you are going to be on unpaid leave for more than 30 days.

Benefits Billing Form

Select whether or not you wish to continue benefits if you go into an unpaid status.
