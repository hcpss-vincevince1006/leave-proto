Applying for a Leave of Absence

Who can apply for a leave of absence?

Leave of Absence applications should be filled out if an employee is missing more than 5 consecutive days of work in a qualifying category.

Qualifing conditions include:

  • Maternity or Paternity
  • Personal Illness
  • Family member illness
  • Military Service
  • Adoption
  • Study/Professional

Find out more about each condition →

How do I apply for a leave of absence?

Each qualifying condition requires a leave of absence request form, Healthcare provider certification form, Maryland State Retirement form, and a benefits billing form.

Contacting your principal or supervisor does not automatically activate, a request for or apporval of, a leave of absence.

Application Requirements

All forms must be completed and received by Human Resources before your leave will be approved.

Request for Leave of Absence form

Complete all personal information and select the type of leave you wish to use.

Healthcare Provider Form

Submit form to your healthcare provider. The form must have a beginning and end date for the duration of incapactiy.

Maryland State Retirement Form

Family member illness is not applicable. Only complete this form if you are going to be on unpaid leave for more than 30 days.

Benefits Billing Form

Select whether or not you wish to continue benefits if you go into an unpaid status.
